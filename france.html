France - The City of Light

Why Visit France?

France is a country that embodies art, culture, and romance. From the iconic landmarks of Paris to the sun-drenched vineyards of Provence, France offers an unparalleled blend of history, gastronomy, and joie de vivre that captivates visitors from around the world.

The iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris
World-renowned French cuisine
The beautiful Loire Valley châteaux

Must-Visit Places

  • Paris - Experience the romance of the City of Light, visit the Louvre, and climb the Eiffel Tower
  • Versailles - Marvel at the opulent Palace of Versailles and its magnificent gardens
  • Loire Valley - Explore fairy-tale castles and taste exquisite wines
  • French Riviera - Enjoy the Mediterranean lifestyle in Nice, Cannes, and Saint-Tropez
  • Mont Saint-Michel - Visit this magical island abbey in Normandy

Unique Experiences

  • Take a cooking class and learn to make authentic French cuisine
  • Go wine tasting in Bordeaux or Champagne regions
  • Visit the lavender fields of Provence
  • Experience Paris's café culture and sidewalk bistros
  • Explore the charming villages of the French countryside

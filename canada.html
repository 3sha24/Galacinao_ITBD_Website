Canada - The Great White North

Why Visit Canada?

Canada is a vast country of stunning natural beauty, diverse cultures, and welcoming people. From the Rocky Mountains to the Northern Lights, from vibrant cities to pristine wilderness, Canada offers endless opportunities for adventure and discovery.

Must-Visit Places

  • Banff National Park - Experience the stunning Canadian Rockies and pristine lakes
  • Niagara Falls - Witness the power of one of the world's most famous waterfalls
  • Vancouver - Explore a city where mountains meet the ocean
  • Quebec City - Immerse yourself in French-Canadian culture and history
  • Churchill, Manitoba - Watch polar bears and the Northern Lights

Unique Experiences

  • Go ice skating on the Rideau Canal in Ottawa
  • Try maple syrup tasting in a sugar shack
  • Watch the Calgary Stampede
  • Go whale watching in the Maritime provinces
  • Experience the vibrant culture of Toronto's diverse neighborhoods
